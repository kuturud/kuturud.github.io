Why Is Apple's M1 Chip So Fast?

On YouTube, I watched a Mac user who had bought an iMac last year. It was maxed out with 40 GB of RAM costing him about $4,000. He watched in disbelief how his hyperexpensive iMac was being demolished by his new M1 Mac Mini, which he had paid a measly $700 for. In real-world test after test, the M1 Macs are not merely inching past top-of-the-line Intel Macs, they are destroying them....
